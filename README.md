# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analysis

##  About This Project
Hi! 
This project is about analyzing business sales data using Power BI to understand how a company is performing.

The main goal was to turn raw data into useful insights that can help a business make better decisions, improve profits, and grow.

---

## What I Focused On
In this project, I looked at:

- Which products generate the most revenue  
- How sales change over time  
- Which categories and regions perform best  
- How profitable the business is  

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI (for dashboard and visuals)  
- Excel / CSV dataset  
- Data cleaning and basic analysis  

---

## 📊 What the Dashboard Shows
The dashboard includes:

- 📈 Sales trends over time  
- 🏆 Top-performing products  
- 📦 Category performance  
- 🌍 Regional analysis  
- 💰 Profit insights  
- 👥 Customer segments  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
Some of the main things I found:

- Technology products bring in the most revenue  
- Sales increase during certain times of the year (seasonal trends)  
- Most sales come from individual (consumer) customers  
- Some areas perform better than others  
- High sales don’t always mean high profit  

---

## Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the business can:

- Focus more on high-performing products  
- Prepare better for peak sales periods  
- Improve performance in weaker regions  
- Reduce unnecessary discounts to increase profit
